# Quadratic Equation Practice

- Source problem: Solve x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0
- Target skill: x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0
- Base difficulty: medium

## Isomorphic

Solve x^2 - 7x + 12 = 0

- Intent: Practice factoring monic quadratics with two distinct integer roots using the same method as the original problem
- Difficulty relation: about_the_same
- Coach note: This problem has the same structure as the original. Find two numbers that multiply to 12 and add to -7.
- Changes:
  - Coefficients changed from -5 and 6 to -7 and 12 while maintaining monic quadratic form and integer factorability
- Answer outline:
  - Factor as (x-3)(x-4)=0
  - Solutions: x=3, x=4

## Easier

Solve x^2 - 3x + 2 = 0

- Intent: Practice factoring very simple monic quadratics with minimal calculation
- Difficulty relation: about_the_same
- Coach note: This quadratic factors easily. Look for two numbers that multiply to 2 and add to -3.
- Changes:
  - Smaller coefficients (-3 and 2 instead of -5 and 6) with simpler integer roots
- Answer outline:
  - Factor as (x-1)(x-2)=0
  - Solutions: x=1, x=2
