# Solved Math Problem

## Problem
解方程 x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0,并说明状态.

## Final Answer
x=2, x=3

## Solution
The quadratic equation $x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0$ factors as $(x-2)(x-3) = 0$, yielding roots $x = 2$ and $x = 3$. The discriminant is $(-5)^2 - 4(1)(6) = 1 > 0$, so the roots are real and distinct.

## Verification
- Exact solver confirms solutions: x = 2 and x = 3
- Both candidates match expected solutions
- Discriminant = 1 (positive), confirming two distinct real roots
