# Quadratic Equation Factoring Practice

- Source problem: Generate isomorphic, easier, and harder follow-up practice variants for x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0.
- Target skill: x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0
- Base difficulty: medium

## Isomorphic

Solve the equation: x^2 - 7x + 12 = 0.

- Intent: To practice identical factoring method with different numerical values
- Difficulty relation: about_the_same
- Coach note: Focus on finding two numbers that multiply to 12 and add to -7
- Changes:
  - Replaced the linear coefficient -5 with -7 and constant term 6 with 12 while maintaining monic quadratic form and integer roots
- Answer outline:
  - Factor as (x-3)(x-4)=0
  - Set each factor to zero
  - Solutions: x=3, x=4

## Easier

Solve the equation: x^2 - 3x + 2 = 0.

- Intent: To build confidence with minimal calculation steps
- Difficulty relation: about_the_same
- Coach note: Emphasize that roots are consecutive small integers
- Changes:
  - Used smaller coefficients (-3 and 2) with simpler integer roots (1 and 2)
- Answer outline:
  - Factor as (x-1)(x-2)=0
  - Set each factor to zero
  - Solutions: x=1, x=2
