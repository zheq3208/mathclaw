## Isomorphic

某质点沿直线运动,位移 $ S $(单位:m)与时间 $ t $(单位:s)之间的关系为 $ S(t) = 3t^3 - 4t^2 $,则当 $ t = 2 \, \text{s} $ 时该质点的瞬时速度为

A. 12 m/s 
B. 16 m/s 
C. 20 m/s 
D. 24 m/s

- Intent: Reinforces derivative calculation with adjusted polynomial coefficients and time point while maintaining identical problem structure.
- Difficulty relation: about_the_same
- Coach note: Ensure students recognize the derivative process remains unchanged despite coefficient variations.
- Changes:
  - Coefficient of $ t^3 $ changed from 2 to 3
  - Coefficient of $ t^2 $ changed from 5 to 4
  - Time value changed from 3s to 2s
- Answer outline:
  - Compute derivative $ S'(t) = 9t^2 - 8t $
  - Substitute $ t = 2 $
  - Calculate $ 9(4) - 8(2) = 36 - 16 = 20 $

## Easier

某质点沿直线运动,位移 $ S $(单位:m)与时间 $ t $(单位:s)之间的关系为 $ S(t) = 3t^2 - 2t $,则当 $ t = 1 \, \text{s} $ 时该质点的瞬时速度为

A. 2 m/s 
B. 3 m/s 
C. 4 m/s 
D. 5 m/s

- Intent: Focuses on foundational derivative concept with linear velocity function to build confidence.
- Difficulty relation: about_the_same
- Coach note: Emphasizes basic power rule application without higher-degree terms.
- Changes:
  - Reduced displacement function to quadratic polynomial
  - Simplified coefficients
  - Time value set to 1s for straightforward calculation
- Answer outline:
  - Compute derivative $ S'(t) = 6t - 2 $
  - Substitute $ t = 1 $
  - Calculate $ 6(1) - 2 = 4 $
