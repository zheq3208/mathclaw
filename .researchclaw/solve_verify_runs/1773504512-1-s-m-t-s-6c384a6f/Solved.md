# Solved Math Problem

## Problem
1. 某质点沿直线运动,位移 $ S $(单位:m)与时间 $ t $(单位:s)之间的关系为 $ S(t) = 2t^3 - 5t^2 $,则当 $ t = 3 \, \text{s} $ 时该质点的瞬时速度为

A. 21 m/s 
B. 61 m/s 
C. 33 m/s 
D. 45 m/s

## Final Answer
21 m/s

## Solution
Instantaneous velocity is the derivative of displacement: $ v(t) = \frac{dS}{dt} = \frac{d}{dt}(2t^3 - 5t^2) = 6t^2 - 10t $. At $ t = 3 $, $ v(3) = 6(9) - 10(3) = 54 - 30 = 21 \, \text{m/s} $.

## Verification
- Correct derivative: $ S'(t) = 6t^2 - 10t $
- Evaluation at $ t=3 $: $ 6*9 - 30 = 21 $
- Matches option A: 21 m/s
- Candidates incorrectly solved $ S(t)=0 $ instead of computing $ v(t)=S'(t) $
